Saleem Asfour

Background

I was born in Jordan on May 22, 1998. I did not move to the United States till I was three years old. We had lived in an apartment in New York City, until the incident of 9/11. We were forced to move out since we were Middle Eastern.

Education

In the beginning my family moved a ton. I lived in four places before we settled down in Yonkers, NY. I attended Primary School at Patricia Dichiaro Elelemntary School. I then moved once more to Syracuse in 2006 and started 4th Grade at Nate Perry Elementary. From 4th Grade to 12th grade I have lived at the same address. I attended Chestnut Hill Middle School and then Liverpool High School.

More

Me and my brother would always horseplay and wrestle, and when I saw an ad about the sport of wrestling, I joined. Wrestling broke up out of my shyness shell; I could actually talk to people without being nervous. But at the end it broke me.
